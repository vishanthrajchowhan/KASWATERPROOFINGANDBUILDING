Water-Proofing in Windows

Our window waterproofing solutions are designed to handle diverse material connections, ensuring your property stays protected from leaks and moisture.

Glass to Metal

Expert sealing where glass meets metal frames, providing a strong, flexible, and durable barrier against elements.

Metal to Metal

Precise waterproofing for metal-to-metal frame joints to ensure long-lasting corrosion protection.

Metal to Concrete

Reliable structural sealing between metal frames and concrete surfaces to prevent water penetration at the perimeter.
